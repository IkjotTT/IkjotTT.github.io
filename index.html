Most Useful/Interesting Elective:

ITM 780 - Web Design and Management

The course that was the most useful and interesting to learn and broaden my understanding was ITM 780 - Web Design and Management. ITM 780 gave me hands on experience in creating and designing website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The course itself is extremely interesting as you get to learn about how websites are created and how complex you can make them using just HTML and CSS. This course helps to teach the basics about web design and becomes a foundation to assist in pursuing a career in web development.
